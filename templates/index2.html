Key Insights

Analysis Summary

  • 36% cancellation rate (9/25 orders) led to ₹69,121 revenue loss — 31.9% of total potential.
  • Corporate customers have the highest cancellation rate at 75%, followed by New customers at 50%.
  • Electronics (₹30,831) and Appliances (₹23,324) contribute the largest individual revenue losses.
  • UPI is the dominant payment method (56% of orders), yet has a 35.7% cancellation rate.
  • Marketplace channel has the lowest cancellation rate (16.7%) vs Website (50%) and App (43.8%).
  • Top cancellation reasons: address not serviceable, out of stock, and price found lower elsewhere (2 each).
  • March had the highest order volume (9 orders) but also the highest absolute cancellations (3).
  • Narsingi and Ameerpet lead with 3 orders each; both have mixed delivery/cancellation outcomes.
